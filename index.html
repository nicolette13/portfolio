Nicolette Richmond's Portfolio

Your website should do more than simply exist — it should communicate and elevate your brand. My name is Nicolette Richmond, I'm based in Miami, Florida, and I'm a web designer that specializes in crafting clean, intuitive interfaces with a strong emphasis on typography, visual hierarchy, and usability. I'll ensure your site appears polished on every screen while functioning seamlessly behind the scenes. Whether you need a business site or a sleek portfolio, I build with scalabilty and clarity in mind.

I have a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with the Bootstrap framework and TailwindCSS. I am also Adobe Certified in Photoshop, and have worked with Illustrator, Animate, and Dreamweaver. I utilize this knowledge to create fast, accessible, fully responsive sites that focus on performance and user experience.

But great development is only half of the equation.

Working with me is a collaborative experience. I take the time to understand your goals, audience, and brand personality. You'll always know where your project stands, what happens next, and why certain decisions are being made. My goal is simple — to deliver a website that aligns with your vision while making the process smooth, efficient, and genuinely enjoyable.
